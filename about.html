BACKGROUND

Over 21 years of experience

Raised in Sydney, Australia, and forever consumed with answering the question: How Does It Work? Leveraging intellectual property for high-growth companies has spanned my entire career.

My academic credentials:

  • Bachelor of Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Laws
  • Master of IP Law
  • Master of Laws

Over 21 years of work experience specialising in intellectual property and technology/startup law:

  • 10 years in Singapore & Hong Kong
  • 11+ years in Australia
  • 14+ years in private practice
  • 7 years in-house: ResMed and Harrison.ai

My professional credentials:

  • Solicitor of the Supreme Court of NSW and Federal Court of Australia
  • Registered Patent Attorney (Australia & New Zealand)
  • Registered Singapore Patent Agent
  • Registered Trade Marks Attorney
  • Fellow of The Institute of Patent and Trade Mark Attorneys of Australia (IPTA)
  • Registered Foreign Lawyer in Hong Kong
  • Member of The Intellectual Property Society of Australia & New Zealand (IPSANZ)
  • Member of Licensing Executives Society
  • Member of AI Ethics Committee at the Australian Computer Society


I specialise in software, machine learning and Web3 technologies, and have extensive experience with medical devices and mechanical devices.


I am available to prepare patent specifications, file patent, trademark & design applications domestically or abroad, and prosecute these applications to issuance.
