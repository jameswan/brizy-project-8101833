Starting your own business but unsure where to start?

Many businesses struggle to find a commercial lawyer & IP specialist with deep technical expertise and extensive startup experience.


Unfortunately, I've seen many founders & entrepreneurs frequently waste time losing momentum and opportunities by personally attempting to navigate the legal, IP, and compliance challenges during their business's crucial early stages. Considering the opportunity cost, it'd be better to focus on leveraging their unique creative strengths: innovation, branding, or sales.


Success is rare without a competent & experienced team to help. How does an entrepreneur discern who can solve the unique challenges their early-stage business faces in a timely and pragmatic way?

In Australia, 60% of new businesses fail within the first 3 years*

Several avoidable factors contribute to this:

  • neglecting to register a trade mark or ensure freedom to operate
  • lacking the right contracts, heightening legal risks and the potential for disputes
  • permanent loss of valuable IP rights
  • operating without an investor-friendly structure

These are a few of the common challenges for new businesses.


* “Why Small Businesses Fail In Australia”, Huffington Post, 28 Sep 2015
